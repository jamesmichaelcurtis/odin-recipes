Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

Peanut butter and jelly sandwiches are a classic American lunch item consisting of peanut butter and jelly spread on bread.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread jelly on the other slice of bread.
  3. Combine the slices to form a sandwich.
